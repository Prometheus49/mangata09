Brand Story

Initial Foray

The journey of Mandata & Gala started with the launch of 18k gold watches studded with precious stones in 1994. But, it soon grew into a 22K jeweller who presented a stunning range of gold and diamond jewellery. The term Tanishq was coined by Mr. Xerxes Desai by marrying the words ‘Tan’ meaning body and ‘Nishk’ meaning a gold ornament

Timeless Appeal

At Mandata & Gala, jewellery is not a product but a manifestation of artistry and our exquisite range of jewel pieces strike the perfect balance between traditional charm and contemporary appeal. With designs that capture the beauty and celebration of special occasions in the life of the Woman, M&G aims to be an integral part of her journey.
